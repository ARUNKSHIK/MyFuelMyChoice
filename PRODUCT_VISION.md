# MyFuelMyChoice - Product Vision

## Mission

**Create the world's most trusted, neutral and transparent platform where people can express their opinion on fuel-related policies through informed voting.**

The platform must never push users toward any particular opinion. Its role is to educate, collect opinions, analyze anonymous trends, and present public insights in a transparent manner.

---

# Core Principles

### 1. Neutrality

The platform must never recommend "Support" or "Oppose."

It only provides verified information and lets people decide for themselves.

---

### 2. Simplicity

A first-time visitor should be able to vote within **10 seconds**.

No mandatory signup.

No unnecessary forms.

No complicated workflow.

---

### 3. Trust

Every important fact should include a reliable source.

Users should always know the difference between:

- Facts
- Public opinions
- Statistical analysis

---

### 4. Transparency

Display statistics openly.

Example:

- Total Votes
- Verified Votes
- Countries Participated
- Last Updated Time

---

# User Journey

```
Home↓What is this platform?↓Learn the Facts        ORContinue to Vote↓Vote↓Thank You↓(Optional)Tell us why you voted this way↓Results Dashboard
```

---

# Homepage Goal

The homepage should answer only three questions.

### What is this website?

A neutral public opinion platform.

### Why should I trust it?

Transparent, source-based, non-political.

### What should I do?

Learn the facts or vote.

Nothing else.

No clutter.

---

# Learn the Facts Page

This page should explain:

- What is ethanol?
- Why is it used?
- Possible benefits
- Possible concerns
- Frequently Asked Questions
- Sources

The tone must remain balanced.

---

# Voting Experience

Three choices only.

👍 Support

👎 Oppose

🤝 Neutral

Voting must feel effortless.

---

# After Voting

Instead of immediately leaving...

Ask one optional question.

> Why did you choose this?

The answer is optional.

This data becomes extremely valuable later.

---

# Results Page

Show:

- Total votes
- Support percentage
- Oppose percentage
- Neutral percentage
- Countries
- Trends over time
- Public comments
- AI-generated summary of common reasons

---

# Design Philosophy

The interface should feel like a mix of

- Apple (clean)
- Google (simple)
- GOV.UK (trustworthy)

No flashy animations.

No unnecessary effects.

Everything should serve a purpose.

---

# Future Vision

Today:

One fuel topic.

Tomorrow:

Any public policy.

Examples:

- Electric Vehicles
- Biofuels
- Road Safety
- Traffic Rules
- Public Transport
- Renewable Energy

Eventually, MyFuelMyChoice becomes a platform for informed public opinion rather than just one survey.

✔ Neutral
