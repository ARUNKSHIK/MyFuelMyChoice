Data Collection

We collect only what is needed to support transparent participation.

Users should always know what data is collected before they participate. Our policy is simple: collect the minimum, explain it clearly, and prefer anonymity.

Collected data

  • Vote choice
  • Optional short reason
  • Timestamp of submission

Not collected

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Address

Transparency

Users are informed before submitting a vote that the platform collects only a choice, an optional reason, and a timestamp. We keep the process understandable and privacy-conscious.
