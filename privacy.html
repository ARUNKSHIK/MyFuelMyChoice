Privacy

Minimal data, clear notice, and anonymous participation.

MyFuelMyChoice is designed to collect only the minimum information needed to run the platform responsibly and transparently.

What we collect

For a vote, we collect only the selected option, an optional short reason, and a timestamp. We do not request your name, email, phone number, address, or government ID.

How we handle it

Anonymous participation is preferred. The simple design helps users understand what is collected before they submit anything. The goal is to support public opinion without collecting unnecessary personal information.

What we do not collect

  • No unnecessary personal information.
  • No sensitive identifiers.
  • No account details for the first vote.
